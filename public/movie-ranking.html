The Best Movies According to Julia

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

K-Pop Demon Hunters

A children's movie in which the songs are currently on repeat on my Spotify.
I recommend to watch it with your family, especially if you have kids.

Avatar: The Way of Water

A marvelous work of art directed by James Cameron. The entire movie keeps you invested
in the story. With breathtaking scenes and an involving script, the two hours movie seems to pass in the blink of an eye

Star Wars: Return of the Jedi

A classic from the theaters. The Return of the Jedi completes the Hero's Journey of Luke Skywalker
putting an end to the Original Triology of the saga with a compelling end.
